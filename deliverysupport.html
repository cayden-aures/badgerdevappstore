What myDeliveryTrack Helps You Manage

myDeliveryTrack is designed to help you manage:

  • delivery shift history and business activity
  • miles, deliveries, pay, tips, and adjustments
  • business expenses and deductible purchases
  • profitability, hourly rate, and business performance
  • historical trends and earning heatmaps
  • future scheduling and recurring shift planning
  • income projections and tax estimates
  • CSV import and export

Common Help Topics

You can contact support for help with:

  • missing, incorrect, or duplicate shift records
  • expense tracking issues
  • schedule or recurring shift problems
  • CSV import or export issues
  • projection or tax estimate questions
  • dashboard or insights issues
  • app crashes or unexpected behavior
  • feature requests

Data and Records

myDeliveryTrack stores your shift, expense, schedule, and planning data locally on your device.
